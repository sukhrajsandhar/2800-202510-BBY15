Porteau Cove

Description

Porteau Cove is a scenic provincial park located along the Sea-to-Sky Highway in British Columbia. It is known for its waterfront campsites, rocky beaches, and stunning views of Howe Sound. Popular activities include scuba diving, stargazing, and quick nature escapes from Vancouver due to its convenient location and natural charm.

Activities

  • Camping
  • Scuba Diving
  • Stargazing
  • Picnicking
  • Wildlife Viewing
